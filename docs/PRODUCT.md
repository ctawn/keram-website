# Product

## Objective

Help Saudi-based Arabic-first customers discover curated journeys and submit qualified enquiries with minimal friction.

## Audience

Saudi families, couples, honeymoon travelers, and residents seeking personally arranged leisure travel. English is fully supported as a secondary locale.

## MVP

- Arabic-first home, collection, journey detail, about, contact, privacy, and terms pages
- English counterparts with LTR layout
- Destination/month discovery entry point
- Enquiry capture and specialist-led conversion
- Supabase-ready persistence with RLS
- Localized metadata, sitemap, robots, responsive and reduced-motion behavior

## Success criteria

- Visitors can reach a relevant journey and enquiry form within three interactions.
- Enquiries record source and locale without exposing customer records publicly.
- Arabic content and RTL layout receive equal design and QA attention.

Live booking, payments, flights, hotels, and supplier inventory are explicitly outside the MVP.
